Interactive Stories

Interactive stories are stories that have alternative branches at different points. Part of our research is to see if making these stories using Microsoft PowerPoint can be therapeatuci. There's currently no easy way to share interactive stories created on PowerPoint that is why we created this website. All you need to do is upload your powerpoint and the research team will review it and in a day or two, it will appear here. You can take down your story at anytime, just email us.
