What are interactive stories? These are narratives with alternative branches at different points, allowing readers to choose their own path through the story.

Our research explores whether creating these stories in Microsoft PowerPoint can be therapeutic. This platform makes it easy to share interactive stories that would otherwise be difficult to distribute.
